# project and may be redistributed freely. No NYT, Broda, or other third-party
# wordlist data was copied to assemble it.

# Format: one ALL-CAPS word per line. Lines starting with `#` are comments.
# Allowed lengths in v0: 3, 4, 5 (the lengths a 5x5 mini fill needs).
#
# Curation goals:
#   - Heavy on vowel-friendly short fill (AREA, OREO, ERA, ETA, OBOE, etc.)
#     because crossword grids live or die on the short crossings.
#   - Common nouns, verbs, and short proper-noun-free fill.
#   - Avoid offensive terms, brand names where possible, and obscure words.
#   - Bias toward letter-pattern diversity over count: a smaller list with
#     varied patterns beats a long list of similar words.
#
# This list will grow as the puzzle quality bar moves up. v0 only needs enough
# breadth for the backtracking fill to find a valid grid on most NBA candidate
# pools.
